Our Work

Creativity and Imagination

A good photographer must be able to look at something ordinary, or even extraordinary, and find a million different ways to interpret what he’s seeing and convey those interpretations in beautiful and meaningful photos.

An Eye for Detail

A good photographer must have a very keen eye for detail to ensure that all elements within the photo—the lighting, the composition, the subject, and everything else in between—work together harmoniously to convey the right vision or message. We focus on the tiniest detail because we believe it can make or break an image.

Story Telling

In one image, does a shot tell the complete story? A picture is worth a thousand words, so one image can capture depth of story. We foucs on creating shots that tell the complete story.

Dynamic Artistery

Camera angle, type of lighting, color, venue, depth, and motion…all such factors will influence the overall outcome of a concept shot. Our focus is to be dynamic and looking for the best shots we can possibly create.

Passion

At Frame Me Photography we believe passion makes the photographer—it will always make you work a little harder, push a little farther, and strive to be better than you were the day before. We are passionate about what we do and this tranlates to our work ethic.
